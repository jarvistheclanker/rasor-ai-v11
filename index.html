Common questions

Everything you want to know

How long does implementation take?+
Most systems are live within 48–72 hours of the discovery call. Complex multi-system builds may take up to a week. We scope everything upfront so there are no surprises.
Do we need to change our existing tools?+
No. We build on top of what you already use — your CRM, your inbox, your scheduling software. No migration required. We plug in, not replace.
Is our customer and business data secure?+
Yes. Your system runs on dedicated infrastructure — not shared servers. Your business data is never used to train public models. We can provide a detailed security overview on request.
What if the AI makes a mistake?+
Every system is built with human-in-the-loop checkpoints for high-stakes actions. Your team approves before anything critical goes out. We monitor and fix issues proactively — usually before you notice them.
Do you offer ongoing support?+
Yes — all plans include ongoing management. As your business changes, your AI adapts. Updates, new automations, and fixes are handled by us in the background.
What industries do you work with?+
We work best with service businesses — med spas, dental offices, HVAC, real estate, and professional services. If your business handles inbound calls or leads, we can automate it.
What does it cost?+
Systems start at $2,500 setup + $697/month managed. What you need depends on your call volume, number of automations, and how deep the integration goes. We scope everything on a 15-minute discovery call — no surprises. No long-term contracts. Month-to-month after setup.
What if I just need one thing automated?+
We work both ways — full systems or a single automation. If there's one process draining your team's time, bring it to the call. We'll scope it and get it built.
